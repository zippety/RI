# Free Image Sources for Reiki Resources

**Top 5 Image Sources** (All free, with license details)

## 1. Wikimedia Commons ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**URL:** https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Reiki

**What's Available:**
- Reiki symbols (public domain)
- Gassho hand positions
- Chakra diagrams
- Educational diagrams
- Historical Reiki images

**License:** Public domain or Creative Commons
**Best For:** Educational use, symbols, historical images

**How to Use:**
1. Search: "Reiki" or "Gassho" or "Reiki symbols"
2. Filter by: License type (public domain preferred)
3. Download: High-resolution images
4. Attribute: As required by license

**Direct Links:**
- Reiki category: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Reiki
- Gassho category: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Gassho
- Reiki symbols: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Reiki_symbols
- Chakras: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Chakras

## License Guide

### Public Domain (CC0)
- ✅ No attribution required
- ✅ Use freely
- ✅ Best for educational materials

### CC BY (Attribution)
- ✅ Attribution required
- ✅ Use freely
- ✅ Credit the creator

### CC BY-SA (Attribution + ShareAlike)
- ✅ Attribution required
- ✅ Must share under same license
- ✅ Good for open-source projects

### Free Commercial Use
- ✅ No attribution required
- ✅ Use for any purpose
- ✅ Pixabay, Pexels, Unsplash

## What Images Do We Need?

### Priority Images
1. **Gassho hand position** - Clear, educational
2. **Reiki symbols** - Cho Ku Rei, Sei He Ki, etc.
3. **Chakra diagrams** - Clear, accurate
4. **Hand positions** - For self-healing guides

### Image Requirements
- **High resolution** (at least 500x500 pixels)
- **Clear and educational**
- **Proper licensing** (free to use)
- **Attribution** (if required)

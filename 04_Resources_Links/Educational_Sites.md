## How to Use These Sites

### For Students
1. Start with one site that resonates
2. Explore their free resources
3. Practice what you learn
4. Join their communities (if available)

### For Teachers
1. Use their resources as teaching aids
2. Share links with students
3. Reference their materials (with attribution)
4. Contribute your own resources

### For Developers/Creators
1. Use their content as reference
2. Create derivative works (check licenses)
3. Attribute properly
4. Share back to community

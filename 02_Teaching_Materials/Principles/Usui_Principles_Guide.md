# Usui Reiki Principles: Teaching Guide

**Free teaching resource for Reiki Principles**

## The Five Principles (Gokai)

### Traditional Japanese

1. **今日だけは怒るな** (Kyo dake wa okoru na) - Just for today, do not anger
2. **今日だけは心配すな** (Kyo dake wa shinpai su na) - Just for today, do not worry
3. **今日だけは感謝して** (Kyo dake wa kansha shite) - Just for today, be grateful
4. **今日だけは業をはげめ** (Kyo dake wa goo hage me) - Just for today, work diligently
5. **今日だけは人に親切に** (Kyo dake wa hito ni shinsetsu ni) - Just for today, be kind to others

## How Usui Taught These Principles

### Daily Practice

1. **Sit in Gassho** (hands together in prayer position)
2. **Recite all five principles** aloud each morning and evening
3. **Reflect on their meaning**
4. **Apply them throughout the day**

### The Gassho Position

- Place palms together at heart center
- Fingers pointing upward
- Elbows slightly relaxed
- Breathe naturally

## Teaching the Principles

### For Beginners

1. **Start with one principle** - Focus on one per day
2. **Practice Gassho** - Show the hand position
3. **Reflect daily** - Morning and evening practice
4. **Be gentle** - "Just for today" means one day at a time

### For Teachers

1. **Teach the Japanese** - Show the original language
2. **Explain the meaning** - What each principle means
3. **Practice together** - Group Gassho practice
4. **Share stories** - Personal experiences with principles

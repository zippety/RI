# Teaching Materials

Educational content for learning and teaching Reiki.

## Folders

- **[Principles/](Principles/)** - Reiki principles and teachings
- **[Guides/](Guides/)** - Step-by-step guides
- **[Templates/](Templates/)** - Reusable templates

## What's Available

- Printable guides
- Teaching outlines
- Student handouts
- Practice templates
- Educational materials

## How to Use

### For Teachers
1. Use guides as teaching aids
2. Share with students
3. Adapt templates for your classes
4. Contribute your own materials

### For Students
1. Download guides for practice
2. Use templates for note-taking
3. Share with others learning Reiki

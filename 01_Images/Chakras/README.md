## What Are Chakras?

**Chakras** are energy centers in the body. The 7 main chakras are:
1. Root (Red)
2. Sacral (Orange)
3. Solar Plexus (Yellow)
4. Heart (Green)
5. Throat (Blue)
6. Third Eye (Indigo)
7. Crown (Violet/Purple)

# How to Download Chakra Diagram Images

## Quick Download Guide

### Step 1: Wikimedia Commons (Recommended)

1. **Go to:** https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Chakras
2. **Browse:** Available chakra diagrams
3. **Filter by License:** Public domain (CC0) preferred
4. **Download:** Click image → "More details" → "Original file" → Download
5. **Save as:** `chakra_[number].png` or `chakra_[description].png`
6. **Place here:** `01_Images/Chakras/` folder
7. **Add attribution:** Create or update README.md with source info

### Step 2: Pixabay (Alternative)

1. **Go to:** https://pixabay.com/images/search/chakra/
2. **Search:** "chakra" or "chakra diagram"
3. **Download:** Click image → "Free Download" → Choose size
4. **Save as:** `chakra_[number].png`
5. **Place here:** `01_Images/Chakras/` folder
6. **Note:** Pixabay images don't require attribution, but it's nice to credit

### Step 3: Add Attribution

Create or update `README.md` in this folder with:

```markdown
## Chakra Images

### Image 1: chakra_1.png
- **Source:** Wikimedia Commons
- **License:** Public Domain (CC0)
- **URL:** [link to original]
- **Description:** [description of image]

### Image 2: chakra_2.png
- **Source:** Pixabay
- **License:** Free for commercial use
- **URL:** [link to original]
- **Description:** [description of image]
```

## What to Look For

**Good chakra images show:**
- ✅ Clear chakra locations (7 main chakras)
- ✅ Color-coded chakras
- ✅ Educational diagrams
- ✅ Simple, clear design
- ✅ Accurate representation

**Image Requirements:**
- **Resolution:** At least 500x500 pixels (prefer 1000x1000+)
- **Format:** PNG (supports transparency) or JPG
- **Quality:** Clear, not blurry
- **License:** Public domain or free commercial use

## Recommended Images

### Wikimedia Commons
- Search: "Chakra" or "Chakra diagram"
- Look for: High-resolution diagrams
- License: CC0 (public domain) preferred

### Pixabay
- Search: "chakra", "chakra diagram"
- Look for: Clear, educational images
- License: Free commercial use (no attribution needed)
